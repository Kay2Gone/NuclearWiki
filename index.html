Welcome to the NuclearAdditions Wiki!

The community-maintained guide to NuclearAdditions — a Minecraft mod that adds nuclear energy, radiation, reactors, and more.

v0.4.2 MC 1.21.1 Forge NeoForge Fabric

NuclearAdditions is a Minecraft mod focused on nuclear energy and its consequences. It adds uranium ore generation, buildable nuclear reactors, a radiation system that affects players and mobs, and a variety of machines for processing nuclear materials. Handle radioactive materials carefully — a meltdown can destroy your base!

This wiki has 98 articles and is actively maintained. Want to help? Learn how to contribute.

Overview

Uranium Ore is a rare ore block added by NuclearAdditions. It generates deep underground in the Overworld, between Y-levels -64 and 0, and emits a faint greenish glow. Mining it without Silk Touch drops 1–3 Uranium Chunks, which can be smelted into Uranium Ingots.

Players standing near unmined Uranium Ore will slowly accumulate radiation. Wearing a full Hazmat Suit negates this effect. Uranium Ore is the primary resource for building Nuclear Reactors and crafting Fuel Rods.

⚠️ Standing near Uranium Ore without protection will give you the Irradiated effect. Make sure to wear a Hazmat Suit while mining!

Crafting

Uranium Ore can be smelted in a Blast Furnace for 2× speed. Four Uranium Chunks can be crafted into a Uranium Block for compact storage.

History

Uranium Ore was added in v0.1.0, the first public release of NuclearAdditions. Its generation parameters were adjusted in v0.3.0 to be slightly rarer but generate in larger veins. The radiation aura was added in v0.2.0.
